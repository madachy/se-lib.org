### San Diego INCOSE Tutorial: Course Portal Problem

A school is building out a course portal where instructors can manage their courses. The minimal viable product for the course portal must have the following functionality:
1. There must be two roles: Instructor and Student
1. Instructors must be able to post lecture material in a repository
1. Students must be able to access lecture material from the repository.
1. Instructors must be able to create multiple choice quizzes that are automatically graded.
1. Student must be able to take quizzes.
1. A grade book will inform students of their grades once released by the instructor.

Using se-lib, start building a model with the following diagrams:
1. Context Diagram
1. Use case diagram
1. Requirements diagram
1. Work Breakdown Structure
